Welcome to ForgetMedNot

ForgetMedNot is a platform that allowes Doctors to add medication reminders to their patients. Patients will receive text reminders to their mobile phone in which they will be prompted to respond by Yes, No, or Snooze.


All responses will be gathered by the system for the doctors to generate reports for analysis and future decision making.

What makes us different from others

Data Privacy & Security

Data security and privacy for us is of the utmost importance. we never track any personal information in ForgetMedNot nor we give your information to any third party application or company. You can use ForgetMedNot without registering your personal information. We conform to all data privacy standards available.

Use Focus

Our primary aim is to provide users clear information regarding their medications, medication adherence and medication reminders. We strive and work towards providing the users with the best experience for it. Quick medicine adherence on the dashboard with detailed adherence reporting.

Offline Use

ForgetMedNot lets you use it as and when you need it. No need to register, no need to be online, no linking to social media. It is fully functional in offline mode without the need for internet connection. Use it at your will and convenience. Get all your reminders without any worries.
