Welcome to ForgetMedNot

ForgetMedNot is a platform that allowes Doctors to add medication reminders to their patients. Patients will receive text reminders to their mobile phone in which they will be prompted to respond by Yes, No, or Snooze.


All responses will be gathered by the system for the doctors to generate reports for analysis and future decision making.
